OUR RESTAURANT

This restaurant is one of the most lovely places:
you can find it between two stores,
the little market “Peppe” and the bigger coloured mobile store “Handy”.
I’m sure you will see it soon,
because at the entrance there are a lot of wonderful flowers and plants.

Awarded Chef

The cook Marco is very creative: thanks to him,
we always taste a lot of different meat,
because the restaurant menu changes weekly.
The meat choice depends on the ingredients availability,
because he assures he serves only fresh dishes.
